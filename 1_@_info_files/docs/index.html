Features

Beautiful Landing Page

Doctors

Full Doctors management with their Departments & Specialities.

Patients

Patient Management with a full history of Cases and Payments.

Nurses

Nurses Management can manage all their day to day activities.

Receptionists

Receptionists login to deal with new Cases and other activities.

Laboratorists

Lab Technicians to do Reports and lab activities management.

Pharmacists

Pharmacists can manage their medicine department activities.

Accountants

Accountants can manage billing and invoices of everything.

8+ User Types

Separate Admin panel for all type of Users with specific permissions.

Appointments

Patient and Doctors appointments management based on availability.

Doctor Schedules

Doctors can set their weekly availability for appointments.

Admin Dashboard

Admin Dashboard with full overview of the whole System.

Payrolls

Manage Payrolls of staff members from all different departments.

Beds Management

Manage the all available Beds with their Bed Types.

Beds Allotments

Blood Bank

Blood Bank Management with available Blood bags.

Blood Donors

Keep a track of Blood Donors with their blood types and donations.

Medicines Categories

Categorize Medicines into different available categories.

Medicines Items

Manage all medicines with it's basic stock inventory.

Document Types

Define the document types that is collected from patients.

Documents

Store all collected document copies at a central place.

Service Packages

Define different available service packages for patients.

Insurance Packages

List all available insurance packages for patients.

Reports

Manage 4 different kind of reports of patients.

Invoices

Create invoices for patients right from the system with PDF Export.

Billing

Billings management of the patient right into the system.

Payment History

Manage all payment history directly in the system.

Admit History

See the full history of patient from admit to discharge.

Operation History

See the full Operation history of the patient.

Prescriptions

Manage all prescriptions given by the doctors digitally.

Ambulance

Ambulance management to keep digital records of visits.

CMS

CMS to handle content of landing page dynamically from backend.

Inquiries

Manage all inquiries done by visitors into the system.
